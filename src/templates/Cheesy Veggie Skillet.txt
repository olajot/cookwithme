<title>
Cheesy Veggie Skillet
<ingredients>
1 head of broccoli that has been chopped into florets,
1 large chopped onion,
3 cloves of minced garlic,
2 diced tomatoes,
1 cup vegan cheese,
1 teaspoon paprika,
1 teaspoon dried oregano,
salt and pepper, to taste,
2 tablespoons olive oil
<steps>
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and saut√© until the onions are translucent. Add the broccoli florets and continue to cook for 5-7 minutes, until the broccoli is tender.
Next, add the diced tomatoes, paprika, and dried oregano to the skillet. Mix well and continue cooking for another 3-4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Sprinkle the vegan cheese over the top of the broccoli and tomato mixture. Cover the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
Serve the cheesy broccoli and tomato skillet hot and enjoy as a satisfying and delicious meal!
