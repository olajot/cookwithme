<title>
Basil Cherry Pineapple Peach Salad
<ingredients>
2 cups fresh basil leaves, 2 cups pitted and halved cherries, 1 cup fresh diced pineapple, 2 pitted and sliced peaches. Optional: honey or balsamic glaze.
<steps>
In a large bowl, combine the basil leaves, cherries, pineapple, and peaches. Toss the ingredients together until everything is evenly mixed. Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator until ready to serve. For extra flavour, you can add a drizzle of balsamic glaze or honey on top. Enjoy this refreshing and flavourful salad that's perfect for a summer day!
